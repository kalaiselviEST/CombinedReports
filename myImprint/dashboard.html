My Commitment

To become carbon neutral by 30 June 2020

  • First , we will measure our carbon profile
  • Second , we will reduce our carbon use year on year, as factor of revenue
  • Third , we will invest in new technology to electrify our plant and machinery

What I'm Going to Do

To reduce our carbon emissions to 1kg/$100 revenue , Due 30/6/21

--Insetting (investing within the company):
  • Sell excavators and trucks older than 2005 and replace with new high efficiency models.
  • Purchase 3 Plug-In hybrids for general runabout use.
  • Invest in mobile excavator crusher.
  • Utilise lime in place of cement for all road stabilisation.

To become carbon neutral, Due 1/12/21

  • Investigate fuel cell options for heavy commercial vehicles; submit application for EECA fund to import one and trial it.
  • Purchase 30 Ha area in sensitive environment, establish QEII covenant, seek funding support and develop planting

To become carbon negative, Due 30/6/22

  • Transition full heavy vehicle fleet to fuel cell.
  • Use low emissions biofuel in all diesel plant
  • Ensure supply chain is monitoring carbon usage and reporting.
